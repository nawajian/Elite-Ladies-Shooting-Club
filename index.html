About Elite Ladies Shooting Club

Introduction

Elite Ladies Shooting Club is a dedicated shooting club established exclusively for women, located in Alor Setar, Kedah, Malaysia. The club provides a safe, comfortable, and professional environment for women aged 18 and above who are interested in learning and developing shooting skills under proper supervision and discipline.

In addition to women-only shooting classes, Elite Ladies Shooting Club also offers a variety of sports and recreational facilities that are open to all genders and age groups, from children to adults. Our facilities include a swimming pool, indoor badminton court, and archery activities, catering to individuals, families, and the wider community.

To participate in shooting classes or to use any of our facilities, participants are required to complete a registration form via Google Form. All registration forms can be accessed directly through our official website.

Elite Ladies Shooting Club is committed to promoting an active lifestyle, confidence, and discipline, while empowering women through high-quality sports and recreational experiences.

Our Vision, Mission & Goals

Our Vision

To be a recognized women's shooting organization that nurtures skilled, confident, and competitive female shooters.

Our Mission

  • To provide systematic training and coaching for women in shooting sports
  • To promote safe and responsible firearm handling practices
  • To encourage women's participation in shooting competitions
  • To develop discipline, focus, and confidence through shooting activities

Our Goals

  • To identify and develop potential female shooters
  • To enhance technical skills through regular training programs
  • To prepare members for competitive shooting events
  • To promote teamwork, sportsmanship, and professionalism
  • To support women's empowerment through sports participation

Our Facilities

Swimming Pool

Four professional-grade badminton courts with proper lighting and flooring. Contact to booking. Regular tournaments organized monthly.

Experience the art of archery with our indoor and outdoor ranges. Suitable for all skill levels with instruction available from certified coaches. Equipment provided for beginners.

How to Register

To join Elite Ladies Shooting Club, please follow these steps:

  1. Complete the online registration form via Google Form (link available on our website)
  2. Visit our club office to submit required documents:
    • Photocopy of IC (Malaysians) or Passport (Foreigners)
    • Two passport-sized photographs
    • Police verification document (will be arranged by the club)
  3. Attend mandatory safety orientation session
  4. Make payment for annual membership fee
  5. Receive your membership card and start enjoying club facilities!
